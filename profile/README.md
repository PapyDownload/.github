## What are these?
Applications to download your favorite stories from pp to EPUB v3 format. Our tools work on **Windows**, **Linux**, **macOS**, and **Android**, **Web App**, **Telegram Bot**.

## Why use PapyDownload Apps?
- **Blazing Fast:** Built with asynchronous Rust, PapyDownload apps are significantly faster than other solutions. Download massive stories in under a minute!
- **True Access**: With a PapyDownload app on your device, you don't have to wait for a website to come back online.
